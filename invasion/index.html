INVASION


This is a project I worked on alone. We got to choose what we wanted to work on in school, so I chose to work in Unreal Engine to gain more experience with it.

In this project, I had the idea of a city being attacked by aliens. I used a city map from Unreal and added a spaceship model to it. I created a few shots and animated the rockets using splines. Then I made a PNG sequence of a kind of monster, which I also placed in the scene, and finally added some sound effects and did post-processing in After Effects.
