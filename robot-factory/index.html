Robot Factory


This is a project I made during the first year of my studies. The idea was to create a looping video of a robot factory in Unity, so that, as a class, we could have a long video for the school's open day.

It was a solo project, so I modeled, rigged, and animated everything in Maya. I also did the texturing in Substance Painter. In Unity, I assembled the scene, added some post-processing, and added some particles.
