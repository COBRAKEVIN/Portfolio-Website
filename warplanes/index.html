Warplanes

In this project, I worked on a scene with a Warplane as hero asset. This was a solo project, where I made forest environment with quixelbridge and modeled and textured the plane from scratch.

The plane is a Martin B-26 Marauder. It was a fast, twin-engine bomber used by the U.S. in World War II. It flew mostly in Europe and the Pacific, dropping bombs on enemy targets. By the end of the war, it became known for accurate bombing and having fewer losses in combat.
