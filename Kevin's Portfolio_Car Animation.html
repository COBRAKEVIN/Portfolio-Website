CAR ANIMATION


This is a project I worked on alone. I always wanted to make a car animation so thats what I did.

In this project, My idea was to create a short car animation in Unreal Engine. I downloaded the car and rigged it in Unreal, then put all the materials on it and downloaded some trees and modeled a bridge which I also placed in the scene, and finally added some sound effects and did post-processing in After Effects.
